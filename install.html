What It Does

Rabbit News is built for quick use. Open it, browse breaking headlines, jump into regional news, search by topic, and read articles in a compact format that works well on the Rabbit screen.

  • Breaking News: fresh headlines on the home screen.
  • Regional News: browse news by region or area.
  • Search: look up topics, countries, events, or people.
  • Reader Mode: open cleaner article pages when possible.
  • Source Fallback: open the original page when a site is not reader-friendly.

Controls And Shortcuts

  • Scroll: move through the headline wheel.
  • Tap: open the selected card.
  • Refresh button: refresh the current screen.
  • Home button: return to the main screen.
  • Side button: go back from cards or article view.
  • Font controls: increase or decrease article text size.
