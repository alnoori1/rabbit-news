Rabbit News is a Rabbit r1 creation that turns the device into a compact news hub.

It gives you:

- Breaking headlines on the home screen
- Regional news browsing
- Search by topic, country, or event
- Clean article reading when extraction works
- Fallback to the original source when needed
- Adjustable reading font size

Controls:

- Scroll to move through headlines
- Tap to open a story
- Refresh button to refresh the current screen
- Home button to return home
- Side button to go back
